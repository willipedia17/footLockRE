FootLockRE's History
FootLockRE is an Indonesian multinational footwear company that has been developing and exploring
new shoes trend. Its branches are spread all over the world. FootLockRE is your gateway to
the Worldwide Shoe Trends. Established in 2007 by Kamado Tanjiro.

Vision
Our vision at FootLockRE is to be the foremost global platform for footwear fashion, connecting individuals with the latest trends and empowering them to express their unique style through exceptional footwear choices. We strive to create a community where fashion enthusiasts can find inspiration, discover new brands, and stay ahead of the curve in the ever-evolving world of shoes.

Mission
FootLockRE only sells the best quality shoes for every customer. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction is unparalleled, as we meticulously curate a diverse collection of shoes that combine style, comfort, and durability.
